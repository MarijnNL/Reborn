Colour codes

For the colour scheme, we used similar colours to that of our logo, the REBORN logo. Our brand is about being adventurous, getting off your comfort zone and trying new things. Based on this we made our logo, mountains in a circle.

1. Represent reality: the colours we use range from different shades and tints of blue that from a far can be percieved as green as well. These colours imitate reality, for example, mountains such as the Kilimanjaro is blue from a distance due to the forest terrain, the blue sky during the day and late sunset, or misty woods.

2. Unity: the colour blue would appeal to people of all ages and genders. By making it neutral, we wanted to show that our brand was/is accepting of everyone irregardless of their gender.

3. Separate: the use of different shades help define the site and logo more.

4. Decorate: In using a soft range of colours, we have managed to properly deocrate ous site without throwing too much as the viewers. The colours gives a calmer feeling which is what we were aiming for.
